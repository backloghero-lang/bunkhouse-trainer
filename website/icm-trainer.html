Why ICM Matters

ICM (Independent Chip Model) turns chip stacks into real-money equity using the payout structure — so the value of a chip isn't linear near the money.

It helps you avoid costly mistakes and make profitable decisions in high-pressure spots: bubbles, pay jumps and final tables.
